### Title
Frontrunning `multicall` via offer consumption causes atomic batch failure — (File: src/Midnight.sol)

### Summary
The `multicall` function in `Midnight.sol` executes multiple calls atomically and reverts the entire batch if any single sub-call fails. An attacker can frontrun a victim's `multicall` containing `take` calls by consuming a small portion of the same offer first (as a different, unprivileged taker), pushing the `consumed[maker][group]` counter past the offer's `maxUnits`/`maxAssets` cap and causing the victim's `take` to revert. This collapses the entire atomic batch.
